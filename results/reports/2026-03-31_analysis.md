## Failure Analysis

|---------|-------|---------------|

| Average gold SQL length | 130 chars | - |

**Root cause:** b2b_saas is undertrained (17% train vs 80% test). Most failures are on simple queries due to column alias mismatches, not complexity.

### Common Failure Patterns

1. **Column alias differences** (e.g., `paid_invoice_count` vs `invoice_count`)
2. **Column selection** (model includes `o.id` when gold doesn't)
3. **ORDER BY variations** (functionally equivalent but hash different)
